Everything you need to know about your SIM-LINE order.

Shipping

We ship from Switzerland within 2 working days of your order. Delivery within Switzerland usually takes 1–2 working days. International shipping is available — please contact us for details.

Returns

If your scarf does not feel right, you may return it unworn within 14 days of receipt. Please contact us first so we can prepare your return.

Care

  • Hand wash in lukewarm water, or machine wash at 30°C in a laundry bag.
  • Use a gentle, colour-safe detergent.
  • Reshape while damp and dry flat, away from direct sunlight.
  • Iron on a low setting if needed.
