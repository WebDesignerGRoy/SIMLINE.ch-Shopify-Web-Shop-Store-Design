The designer

A life with fabrics.

Fabrics have been part of my life since childhood. My grandmother and my mother created with their hands — and this is exactly what I continue today.

Simona

SIM-LINE was born from a simple wish: to create scarves that bring joy every single day. Pieces that are light enough for a summer breeze, soft enough to wear for hours, and beautiful enough to feel a little special.

Every scarf begins with a fabric search. I travel, I look, I touch — and when I find something that moves me, I bring it back to the atelier. There, the fabric meets scissors, thread and time. A lot of time.

Because each fabric is used for only 1 to 3 scarves, no two SIM-LINE pieces are ever the same. When yours is sold, it is gone. That is the promise — and the gift — of a true unique piece.

I hope your scarf becomes a quiet favourite in your wardrobe. Something you reach for often, that travels with you, that softens with age.

Simona
